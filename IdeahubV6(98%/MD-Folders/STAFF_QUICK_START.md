# IdeaHub — Quick Start (Staff & Owner)
**One page · Daily use · Login: your shop URL → Login**

---

## For the owner (2-minute read)

IdeaHub tracks **who is in the shop**, **what they ordered**, and **how they paid (Cash or GCash)**.  
At end of day, **Admin → Daily Balance → Export PDF** gives you a report for your accountant.  
Staff use the steps below; you review **Checkout Records** and **Daily Balance** anytime.

---

## Every shift — 4 steps

### 1. Log in
- Open the IdeaHub link in Chrome/Edge.
- Enter username and password.
- You land on **Dashboard** (active customers).

### 2. Check in a customer (+ button)
- Tap **+** → fill **name**, **people count**, pick **space** (Regular / Premium / Boardroom).
- Tap **Check In**.
- If it says **full**, that lounge has no seats left—pick another space or wait.

### 3. Add food or drinks (if they order)
- Open **Orders** (top menu) or the customer’s order screen.
- Add items from the menu.
- Gray / “Not Available” items cannot be ordered—tell admin to enable them.

### 4. Checkout when they leave
- On **Dashboard**, open the customer → view bill → **Checkout**.
- Choose **Cash** or **GCash** (ask the customer first).
- Confirm in the popup.
- Optional: open **receipt** for the customer.

**Rule:** Always pick the **real** payment method. GCash is not the same as Cash on reports.

## Where to find things

| I need to… | Go to… |
|------------|--------|
| See who is inside now | **Dashboard** |
| Past bills & Cash/GCash | **Checkout Records** |
| Kitchen / order list | **Orders** |
| Boardroom schedule | **Boardroom Booking** |
| Update menu or stock | **Menu** / **Inventory** (staff & admin) |
| Log money spent today | **Expenses** |
| Customer owes money | **Receivables** → mark **Paid** when settled |

**Admin only:** Daily Balance · Analytics · Admin Panel · Reservations

---

## Common problems

| Problem | What to do |
|---------|------------|
| Can’t check in — “full” | Space is at capacity; use another lounge or fewer people. |
| Item not on order screen | Admin must **enable** it under **Menu**. |
| Checkout button missing | Customer may already be checked out—check **Checkout Records**. |
| Wrong payment on report | Payment is set **at checkout**; cannot change after without admin help. |
| Page looks old after menu change | Wait a few seconds or refresh the page. |

---

## End of day (staff)

- Finish or hand off any **active** customers (all checked out).
- Tell admin if any **Cash vs GCash** felt wrong during the shift.

## End of day (owner / admin)

1. **Checkout Records** — spot-check totals; filter Cash / GCash if needed.  
2. **Daily Balance** — pick today’s date → **Generate Report** → **Export PDF**.  
3. Save PDF for records or send to accountant.

---

## Need help?

- **Password / login:** Admin Panel → users.  
- **Prices / menu / capacity:** Admin.  
- **System down:** Restart app or contact your technical support.

*IdeaHub — check in → order → checkout (Cash or GCash) → done.*
